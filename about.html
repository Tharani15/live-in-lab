About Us

At Gastro Pulse, we are committed to transforming the way people experience food delivery by merging health-conscious dining with eco-friendly practices. Our mission is to revolutionize dietary management and tackle the pressing issue of food waste through innovative solutions and sustainable strategies.

We believe that food should not only nourish the body but also contribute positively to the planet. By integrating smart demand forecasting and precise portion control, our platform ensures that every meal is tailored to actual consumer needs, reducing unnecessary food preparation and minimizing waste.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for personalized home-cooked meal delivery, empowering users to achieve healthier lifestyles while reducing their environmental footprint. We aim to create a future where every meal is prepared with intention, minimizing waste and maximizing the benefits to both people and the planet.

Our Commitment

  • Health & Wellness: Personalized meal recommendations tailored to your dietary needs and health goals.
  • Sustainability: Smart demand forecasting, portion control, and surplus food donation to reduce waste.
  • Community Support: A platform that fosters connection, allowing users to share, donate, or resell meals.
  • Local Empowerment: Supporting local chefs and food producers, promoting a robust local food system.
